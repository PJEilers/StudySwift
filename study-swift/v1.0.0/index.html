Welcome to StudySwift

Hi there!
We are StudySwift and we are here to help you study even faster than before. We are doing this by enabling you to quickly summarize whatever you are reading. The idea is simple, you copy a piece of text to the field on the left and start reading. As soon as you pass a sentence you want to save just click on it. We'll save this sentence in the box at the right, which will be your summary.
